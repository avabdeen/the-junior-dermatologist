My Medical Ambition & Goals

A couple of years ago I knew a career in medicine was my calling, and combining that with my everlasting passion for skin health, finding dermatology was my hail mary! I hit puberty the earliest in year 5, with my acne filled skin standing out from the young, clear skin of the other girls. This riddled me with insecurities, and gave me a harsh mental battle from a young age. Some may think that skin health is just a cosmetic, surface level thing, that only impacts your appearance. However, it goes deeper than that, deep in a way that troubles a large percentage of the global youth. So if I could be apart of the doctors that can help these people who struggle, my life would feel complete.
